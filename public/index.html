Goal:
Your goal is to get as big as possible. You can kill people that are smaller than you, but look out if they have the bomb powerup they can still kill you! Look out for mines and bullets as they harm you.
Controls:
↑ ↓ / W S - Move forward/backward
← → / AD - Turn left/right
SPACE - Drop mine (if you have one)
T / Left mouse - Shoot bullet (costs 25 mass, you steal 25 mass when you hit the other.)
ENTER - Start/send chat message
